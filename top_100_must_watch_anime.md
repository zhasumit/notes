# Top 100 Must-Watch Anime - Essential List

## Top 10 - Absolute Must-Sees

- [x] 2. **Death Note** - Psychological thriller that hooks everyone, even non-anime fans
- [x] 3. **Attack on Titan** - Revolutionary storytelling and animation that changed anime
- [x] 4. **Spirited Away** - Studio Ghibli masterpiece and gateway to anime films
- [x] 5. **One Piece** - The ultimate adventure anime (start with East Blue saga)
- [x] 6. **Demon Slayer** - Stunning animation and emotional storytelling
- [x] 7. **My Hero Academia** - Modern superhero anime done perfectly
- [ ] 8. **Cowboy Bebop** - Timeless space western with incredible music
